About

Building Practical 3D Vision Systems

I'm a computer vision researcher and M.S. student at Dongguk University. My work focuses on monocular SLAM, multi-view stereo, and depth estimation, with a goal of deploying robust 3D modeling in real-world environments.

Dongguk University Computer Vision 3D Reconstruction

Research Focus

I explore how to bridge real-time modeling and high-quality offline reconstruction. Recent efforts include novel view selection for Gaussian Splatting and diffusion-based inpainting for missing geometry.

Previously, I studied Software Engineering at Hanyang University and worked as a research intern and data scientist at AI Image, RTM.
