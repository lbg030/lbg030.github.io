About

I'm a computer vision researcher and M.S. student at Dongguk University focusing on monocular SLAM, multi-view stereo, and depth estimation. My interests span both real-time online modeling and high-quality offline reconstruction.

Previously I studied Software Engineering at Hanyang University and spent time as a research intern and data scientist at AI Image, RTM. I enjoy building practical 3D vision systems that bridge research and field applications.
